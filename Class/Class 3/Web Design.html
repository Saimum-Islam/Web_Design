ABOUT NUB AND OUR HISTORY
Northern University Bangladesh (Bengali: নর্দান বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়) or NUB is a leading private university in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was established in 2002. The university was sponsored and founded by International Business Agriculture & Technology (IBAT) Trust.In 2002, Northern University Bangladesh was established by International Business Agriculture & Technology (IBAT) Trust, a registered, non-political, non-profit voluntary organisation, NUB made its foray in to the field of higher education and established itself as a center for excellence.
